About Me

I help startups and companies develop amazing brands, websites and internet applications

Dear Reader, yes you,

I hope this message finds you well. I wanted to drop you a note all the way from Accra, Ghana, where I'm coding my way through the web development universe. It's a Kenyan developer's take on "Ghana, Here I Come!" – pun intended.

For the past five years, I've been on a digital adventure, creating websites and web applications that have more lines of code than a novel. Startups, companies, and even individuals have all been part of my journey. If websites could talk, mine would have some epic stories to share.

Currently, I'm at MEST, brewing up a web application that helps entrepreneurs across Africa make their dreams come true. It's like giving them the digital superpower to grow their businesses, one line of code at a time.

In my spare time (yes, developers have that too), I've been playing founder and co-founded two startups. One's called Lipana, where we make financial payments smoother than a jazz saxophone solo. The other's Aircart, bringing creativity and tech together – think of it as the Picasso of the digital world.

In short, my life revolves around code, startups, and lots of coffee. Thanks for listening to my tech tale, and if you ever need a hand with anything web-related, don't hesitate to reach out. After all, what are fellow developers for, if not to share a laugh over lines of code?

Stay digital, stay epic!

Best regards, Shad

P.S. Keep your debugging skills sharp and your humor even sharper.
